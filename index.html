Log session to Google Sheets

Connect Google Sheets

One-time setup — takes about 2 minutes. The app will write directly to your Sheet after each session.

  1. Open your Google Sheet
  2. Click Extensions → Apps Script
  3. Paste the SheetLogger.gs script (provided separately)
  4. Click Deploy → New deployment → Web app
  5. Set Execute as: Me & Who has access: Anyone
  6. Click Deploy, authorise, then copy the Web app URL
